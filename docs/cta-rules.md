# CTA Rules

## Global

- CTA is never automatic for every post.
- CTA must sound natural and contextual.
- Avoid hard-sell wording, urgency bait, and landing-page style copy.
- `auto_publish=false` remains the default.

## night_scout

- CTA type: `LINE_AND_DM`
- Allowed CTA:
  - `詳しく聞きたい子はLINEかDMで相談してね`
  - `店選びで迷ってる子はLINEかDMで聞いてね`
- Avoid:
  - aggressive recruiting
  - suspicious income claims
  - generic motivational copy

## liver_manager

- CTA type: `LINE_AND_DM`
- Allowed CTA:
  - `配信の伸ばし方を相談したい人はLINEかDMで聞いてね`
  - `数字の見方で迷う人はLINEかDMで相談してね`
- Avoid:
  - agency sales tone
  - guaranteed-income claims
  - other-company criticism

## beauty_account

- CTA type: `NONE`
- No real posting.
- No queue promotion to `READY` or `POSTED`.
- Medical, drug, before/after, and beauty efficacy claims require review.
